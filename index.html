About Me

Hi! My name is Amrit Dhaliwal

I am a passionate software developer with a strong interest in creating innovative solutions through code. From front-end development to back-end systems, I enjoy the entire software development process and constantly strive to improve my skills. Whether it's building dynamic web applications, optimizing user experiences, or learning new programming languages, I embrace every challenge as an opportunity to grow.

Beyond programming, I have a deep love for reading, particularly non-fiction books that expand my knowledge and perspective. I've completed The Psychology of Money, The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, How to Win Friends and Influence People, and many more. These books have shaped my mindset, helping me develop a disciplined and growth-oriented approach to both work and life.

In addition to coding and reading, I have a creative side that I express through stop-motion animation. I love bringing ideas to life frame by frame, blending my technical skills with artistic storytelling. This combination of logic and creativity keeps me engaged, whether I'm building software or crafting animations.
